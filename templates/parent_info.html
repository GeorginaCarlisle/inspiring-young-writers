Information for Parents

Please read through the information below to see how this platform works, the steps we have put in place to increase safety, and what you and your child can do to keep themselves and others safe on the platform.

Your child's profile

When using the platform, your child will be referenced by their pen name and age. No other identifying information will be used on the platform.

Their pen name should not be their real name.

Your child's age will be used so that other children using the platform are able to identify the writing produced by someone older or younger than them and respond appropriately.

Signing up and logging in

Only you and your child will be able to access their account using their pen name and a password.

Please help your child to create a strong password.

When signing up, you will need to provide your (not your child's) first name and last name as well as your email address. This is so that we can contact you should the need arise.

Published work

Your child will have the option to submit their writing to be published on the platform for other children to read.

When submitted their work will be sent to be approved by our Admin, who will read through and check that it is suitable to be read by other children.

Nothing can be published to the platform without the approval of our Admin.

We would appreciate it, if you could have a quick glance over anything your child writes before they submit. Please also support your child to understand that it may take a few days before their work is published.

If any changes to their work are needed before approval can be granted, then a message will appear within the 'My Work' section of their account and you will alsoe be notified by email. PWe would appreciate if you could then support your child in understanding any changes to be made.

Raising Concern

If you should have any concerns then please contact us using the contact form.
